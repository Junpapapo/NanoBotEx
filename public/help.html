Intro & Installation

⚠️ Note: This help page may contain features that are currently under development. Please note that there may be functional differences and update gaps between the official Chrome Web Store version and the development version.
💡 Requirement: NanoBot AI relies on Chrome's built-in local AI model (Gemini Nano). Running it requires Chrome version 128+ and a one-time configuration. Please refer to our FAQ & Q&A page for step-by-step guidance.

NanoBot AI is an innovative, fully on-device personal assistant that runs directly in your Chrome browser. It works without sending any conversation data to cloud servers, delivering ultra-private, local intelligence directly on your own computer.

💡 Tip: After installing, click the Extensions icon (puzzle piece) in the Chrome toolbar and Pin NanoBot for quick access anytime.

Getting Started

1-Click Sidepanel
Click the NanoBot icon in the toolbar, then select [Open Sidepanel] to enjoy a spacious sidebar layout for long, immersive interactions with the AI.
Manual Engine Test
Click the [AI ON] status badge in the top right of the popup to run a 1-second diagnostic check on your browser's local Gemini Nano engine.
Custom Avatar & Name
Use the carousel arrows to select your AI assistant's avatar, and type in a custom name to tailor your experience.

On-device AI Features Core

⚠️ Important Note (Usage Restrictions):
  • Due to Chrome's strict security policies, content scripts are blocked on internal Chrome pages (e.g., chrome://extensions, New Tab Page) and the Chrome Web Store. Please test drag functions on standard HTTP/HTTPS websites.
  • Immediately after installing or updating/rebuilding the extension, you must reload (F5) existing browser tabs to activate the floating drag action tooltip.
100% Offline Chat
Since the model runs natively on your machine, your conversations continue even when you lose internet connectivity.
Context Sharing
Summarize and ask questions about the webpage you are currently reading, boosting your study, writing, and coding productivity.
👑 Real-Time Web Search Premium
Click the globe (🌐) icon in the chat input bar to toggle real-time search modes. [AUTO] mode automatically detects real-time keywords (e.g., weather, stocks, news) to search only when needed. [LIVE] mode forces live search on every question to provide up-to-date answers.
👑 Smart Drag & Quick Actions Premium
Highlight any text on a web page to evoke the 👑 Summary/Translation floating tooltip. Click it to open the sidepanel and process content instantly.
👑 1-Click Tab Summary Premium
Click the document summary icon in the chat header to scrape and generate a concise 3-line summary of the current active browser tab.
Avatar Shuffle
Click the shuffle icon to randomly select from a collection of fun, unique avatars for your assistant.

Lingo Tutor (Language Learning) Learning

NanoBot supports the 'Daily Learning Expression (Lingo Tutor Card)' service, where a friendly and intelligent AI teacher provides customized language expressions and detailed explanations.

🎓 Tutor Panel & Live Expressions
Click the Academic Cap (🎓) icon in the sidebar toolkit to open the Lingo Tutor settings. Click the Academic Cap (🎓) request button at the top-right of the panel to instantly request a tailored learning expression card. Expand the collapsible Tutor Settings card to modify language, topic, age/level, and difficulty at any time.
🌐 8 Languages & Custom Topics
Supports 8 major languages: English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, and Vietnamese. Tailor the AI teacher's tone according to diverse topics (Daily, Travel, Business, Grammar) and demographics (Kids/Teens/Adults).
⏰ Daily Learn Reminders (Scheduler)
Enable daily learn reminders and set a preferred delivery time (AM/PM). The background scheduler triggers an OS push notification at the specified time, and automatically delivers a live typing expression card upon opening the chatbot workspace.
📖 Review Archive (Saved Expressions)
Every generated learning card is automatically saved to the local review archive. Play native audio (TTS) pronunciation, check full dictionary word definitions, and delete cards within the review list panel.
✨ Emerald-Glow Lingo Card Structure
The generated learning expressions are beautifully presented in a special emerald-glow bordered card consisting of the following key elements:
  • Expression of the Day: The raw sentence in the target language (displayed in bold emerald text with a speaker icon for TTS).
  • Pronunciation & Translation: Phonetic guidance/reading tips along with the native translation.
  • Mini Vocabulary Badge: A grid-styled block highlighting key words and their definitions at a glance.
  • Teacher's Notes: Warm, conversational explanations in a chalkboard-like panel giving structural tips and real-life context.

Settings & Theme Customization

  • Localization: NanoBot supports English, Korean, and Japanese out of the box, translating both the popup and sidepanel.
  • Skin Modes: Switch between a premium, custom Dark Mode and a crisp, clean Light Mode in one click.
  • Theme Colors: Choose from 5 sleek gradient color schemes (such as Indigo or Violet) to match your visual taste.

Security & Privacy

100% Local Operations
All chat histories, prompt configurations, and settings are stored locally on your device (IndexedDB/LocalStorage). No data is ever sent to external cloud servers.
